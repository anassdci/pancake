How to Make Pancakes


Weekend mornings are made for pancakes, but getting them to turn out just right can be stressful―especially when you’re cooking for a crowd. Whether you’re starting from scratch or using a mix, try the simple how-tos in this video for perfect pancakes. Whether you’re starting from scratch or using a mix, try the simple how-tos in this video for perfect pancakes.

1-Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat until creamy
2-Melt the butter in a microwave-safe bowl.
3-Add the butter and milk to the mix.
4-Cook for about two minutes or until the pancake is golden.
5-Enjoy!


Ingredients



The following ingredients will make about 8-10 inch (25 cm) pancakes (more or fewer, depending on the size); you may change the amounts of ingredients according to the number you wish to serve:

  • 2 cups (18oz/510g) self-rising or all-purpose flour.
  • 2 or 3 eggs.
  • 1 1/2 cups (350ml) of milk.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda.
  • 2 tablespoons of butter or vegetable oil.
  • 5 tablespoons of sugar.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional).
  • A pinch of salt.
